Financial Formula: ROE

Return on Equity (ROE) is a key measure of profitability.

\[ \text{ROE} = \frac{\text{Net Income}}{\text{Shareholder Equity}} \]

For Q2:

\[ \text{ROE} = \frac{\$9.8M}{\$65.0M} \approx 15.08\% \]
